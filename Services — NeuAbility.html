Services

At NeuAbility, we provide clinically informed adaptive exercise sessions, integrative health, and community events.

Our evidence-based programs are personalized to each client’s needs, goals, and preferences.

We promote greater independence and confidence through empowerment and community support.

Adaptive Exercise

Adaptive Exercise Sessions

Adaptive Exercise Sessions are tailored to each member’s needs and goals, with a focus on long-term health and wellness. Our trainers provide individualized support by responding in real time to each person’s body, tone, fatigue, and capacity. Through teamwork and trust, adaptive exercise helps build physical ability, confidence, and a deeper connection between trainer and member.

$85 per session (60 minutes)
$800 10-pack of sessions (60 minutes each)
$125 per session (90 minutes)

Rise & Walk

The Rise & Walk is an advanced, 3-in-1 robotic rehabilitation station designed for physical and neurological therapy. Developed by Healing Innovations, it helps individuals with mobility or neurological impairments (such as spinal cord or traumatic brain injuries) regain mobility by facilitating repetitive stepping, seated exercises, and balance training.

$85 per session (60 minutes)
$225 for 3 session trial pack

FES Bikes

We offer a monthly membership for FES (Functional Electrical Stimulation) bike rides for only $30 a month. Schedule a 60 minute session with us any day of the week or everyday of the week during business hours. Some benefits of the FES bike is muscle preservation, increased circulation, bone health, spasticity management, and more.

$30 per month

Adaptive Circuit Classes

For only $30/Month, get a high intensity 60 minute, stationed based group workouts everyday of the week. We like to include upper body exercises that hit all of the upper body muscles along with adding cardiovascular training utilizing various equipment that are safe for all injury levels and circumstances creating a fun community centered environment

$30 per month

Integrative Health

Massage Therapy

A 60-minute massage can help lessen spasms and tone, as well as increase your range of motion. Our dedicated team of massage therapists have a passion for working with our community and have developed skills unique to addressing our members’ needs.

$85 per session / Covered by CIH Waiver

Acupuncture

Acupuncture uses small needles to affect bioelectrical signaling in the body. In doing so, physiological processes can be initiated. For instance, getting the body into a parasympathetic state so it can relax and heal, or release endogenous opioids for your body’s own pain relief. 

$85 per session / Covered by CIH Waiver

Chiropractic

We at NeuAbility make chiropractic as accessible as possible. We have a fantastic team of chiropractors that we work hand in hand with over at Dorsa Chiropractic. They are located in the same parking lot as us and will come to NeuAbility for adjustments and/or to their facility down the way

$70 per session / Covered by CIH Waiver
